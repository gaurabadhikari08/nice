Overview

Grace International Hospital is a leading medical facility located in the heart of the city, serving patients in the surrounding areas and beyond. Founded in 1965, the hospital has a long history of providing high-quality medical care to the community.
As a non-profit hospital, Grace is dedicated to meeting the healthcare needs of its patients while also giving back to the community through outreach programs and charitable initiatives. The hospital is part of a larger healthcare network, which allows it to offer patients access to a wide range of medical specialties and advanced treatments.
Grace International Hospital is staffed by a team of highly trained and experienced medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and other healthcare specialists. The hospital offers a full range of medical services, from primary care and preventive medicine to advanced diagnostics and specialized treatments.
Some of the hospital's key specialties include cardiology, neurology, oncology, and orthopedics. The hospital also has state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment facilities, including a full-service imaging center and a modern surgical suite.
At Grace International Hospital, patient care is the top priority. The hospital is committed to providing compassionate, personalized care to each and every patient, with a focus on achieving the best possible outcomes. With a strong reputation for excellence in medical care, Grace Hospital is a trusted provider of healthcare services in the community.
